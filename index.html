Oktoberfest

Oktoberfest is the world's largest Volksfest and will take place in Munich between Saturday 19th September and Sunday 4th October next year. We will reserve a table to get the best experience possible!

Ingolstadt Volksfest

In order to maximise our Oktoberfest experience without breaking the bank, I suggest we also visit Ingolstadt, the city where I will have spent 4 years of my life, where there is also a Volksfest around the same time, with a cosier vibe and cheaper beer.
